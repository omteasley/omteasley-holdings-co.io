About O.M. Teasley Holding Company

O.M. TEASLEY Holdings helps businesses get the money they need, when they need it. We keep the loan process simple, fast, and honest, so our clients know exactly what to expect. O.M.T. is here to support long-term growth.

Our Story

Founded with integrity at its core, O.M. TEASLEY Holdings helps businesses get the money they need—when they need it most. Known for making the loan process simple, fast, and honest, O.M.T. has positioned itself as a trusted financial partner for entrepreneurs and business owners navigating complex funding needs.

A Name Built on Integrity

The company’s story begins with Onyx Teasley, a seasoned professional in the investment sector who found himself increasingly drawn to the world of money management. While managing company loans at a prestigious investment firm, Onyx saw firsthand how businesses often struggled to secure transparent, timely funding. Frustrated by the red tape and impersonal systems that plagued traditional lenders, he saw a clear opportunity: create a company that does things differently.

One day, while walking and reflecting on his next move, Onyx noticed a common thread among successful financial institutions—the use of family names. After a conversation with his brother, the decision was made. The company wouldn’t just be about money; it would be about trust, character, and legacy. Thus, O.M. TEASLEY Holdings was born—a name representing not just a person, but a promise.

Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To deliver straightforward, reliable funding solutions that empower businesses to grow with confidence.

Our Vision

To reshape how business owners experience financing—replacing confusion with clarity, delay with speed, and doubt with trust.

Our Values

  • Transparency – Clear terms, no hidden fees, no surprises.
  • Integrity – Lending solutions that reflect the values behind the family name.

  • Partnership – A long-term mindset focused on client success beyond the first loan.

What O.M.T. Offers

O.M. TEASLEY Holdings specializes in tailored loan solutions for small to mid-sized businesses across various industries. Services include:

  • Working capital loans
  • Equipment financing
  • Short-term bridge loans
  • Expansion and project-based funding

Whether a business is looking to stabilize cash flow, purchase new assets, or seize an unexpected growth opportunity, O.M.T. delivers fast access to capital without the bureaucracy of traditional banking.

Serving Businesses Nationwide

While rooted in the founder’s personal values, O.M. TEASLEY Holdings serves a nationwide clientele. The company works with entrepreneurs, service providers, retailers, contractors, and a wide array of other small business owners who need smart, responsive lending solutions without the red tape.

What Sets O.M.T. Apart

  • Speed Without Sacrificing Service: Funding decisions are made quickly, but never at the expense of client clarity or care.
  • Real Relationships: Clients don’t get passed off to a call center—they get real conversations with people who understand business finance.
  • Ethical Lending: The company only offers loans that make financial sense for the client’s situation—no high-pressure tactics, ever.

Looking Ahead

O.M. TEASLEY Holdings is focused on long-term growth—for its clients and itself. Plans for the future include expanding service offerings, integrating more financial advisory support, and investing in digital tools that make the lending process even more accessible.

As it continues to grow, the company remains anchored to the vision Onyx Teasley had from day one: build a business his family name would be proud of, and use it to help others build legacies of their own.

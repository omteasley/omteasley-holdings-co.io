Discover Our Business Funding Solutions

O.M.T. provides a diverse range of financial products tailored to meet the unique needs of your business, from day-to-day operations to long-term strategic growth.

Business Term Loans

Lump-sum capital with fixed or structured repayments, providing stability for planned investments or large expenses.

Business Lines of Credit

Enjoy revolving access to funds up to a set limit, paying interest only on the amount you use, perfect for managing ongoing cash flow.

Equipment Financing

Dedicated loans for purchasing or upgrading essential business equipment, often secured by the equipment itself.

Merchant Cash Advances (MCAs)

Get an upfront advance against your future receivables, repaid seamlessly via daily or weekly sales holdbacks.

Working Capital Loans

Short-term financing designed to cover your immediate operational expenses such as payroll, rent, and supplies.

Consolidation Loans

Simplify your finances by combining or refinancing multiple existing debts into one manageable repayment plan.

Unsecured Options

Many of our funding solutions do not require collateral, simplifying the application process and speeding up access to funds.

Flexible Terms

Our loan structures are designed to match your business's unique cash flow cycles, ensuring manageable and comfortable repayments.
